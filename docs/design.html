Do's and Don'ts

✓ Do

  • Keep one primary button per view — the next step should be unambiguous.
  • Pair every severity color with its icon and label.
  • Use mono for all technical evidence — URLs, errors, statuses.
  • Lead result screens with the verdict banner and stat numbers.
  • Keep the public report page document-plain — it represents the agency.

✗ Don't

  • Use severity colors decoratively (no red delete buttons).
  • Introduce new hues, gradients, or improvised dark modes.
  • Animate attention — no pulse, shake, or bounce on findings.
  • Fill idle space with decorative charts or stat wallpaper.
  • Put more than one blue feature card on a screen.
